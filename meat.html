How to cook steak

Ingredients

  • A pan or barbecue
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper
  • butter

Steps

  1. Rub the steak along with some olive oil, sea salt and black pepper
  2. Place the steak in a pan to a medium temperature for 6 minutes, constantly flipping it
  3. Use butter to rub on the steak to give it a better taste
  4. Once it has been cooked, place if finally on a plate and let it rest for 2 minutes
